Welcome :) I'm Michael Bishop - husband, father of three and Methodist minister working in a local church in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa.

A five on the Enneagram, my natural default is to observe and reflect (the name of the blog points to the reflection practice of St Ignatius). I also feel a compulsive need to gather information and resources - the thought of lessons being learned somewhere and not being recorded makes me feel uneasy :) So maintaining this site is mostly free therapy for me ... but if you find anything helpful here, that will be great too!

At the moment, the site is mostly a blog - many of these entries are also published through our church website - but I will also be adding more video resources - a combination of spirituality / training resources and tech tutorials.

You can contact me via Twitter or by commenting on the site (once the comment system is working!).
